Kasha~Lazy Cabbage Rolls

Kasha, or lazy cabbage rolls, is a casserole made with all the staples of a cabbage roll, but with a fraction of the time and effort. You can find plenty of recipes for lazy cabbage rolls online, but ours is a vegetarian version (our family has a variety of food allergies and preferences, so most recipes are vegetarian as a default), and in my opinion, the ultimate comfort food. Sweet and savoury tomato sauce mixed in with cabbage and rice, it makes a lovely addition to a holiday meal, and adds a little twist to your regular fare.

Ingredients

  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 head cabbage (or more), chopped
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 can diluted tomato soup
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • Dill to garnish

Directions

  1. Mix together all ingredients in a lerge (2 qt) casserole dish.
  2. Cover with water.
  3. Bake (covered) at 325F for 3 hours, stirring occasionally, then check cabbage to see if done.
